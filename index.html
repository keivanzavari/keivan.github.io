Biography

Keivan Zavari was born May 4, 1984 in Khorramabad, Iran. He received his B.Sc. degree in electrical engineering from Iran University of Science and Technology and his M.Sc. degree in control engineering from Tarbiat Modares University, in 2007 and 2009, respectively. Currently he is a PhD researcher at Meco research group in Mechanical Engineering Department of KULeuven.
